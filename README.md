# Relationship Copilot

**Solving Relationships for Everyone**

## 🎯 Vision

We believe relationships are the foundation of human experience — yet they're increasingly complex in our digital age. Whether it's connecting with a friend, collaborating with a colleague, finding a romantic partner, or building meaningful connections with AI agents, relationships require understanding, effort, and the right tools.

**Our Mission**: To make every relationship better through intelligent design, gamification, and real-time connection.

## 🌟 Core Philosophy

- **Universal Applicability**: Works for human-to-human, human-to-agent, and agent-to-agent relationships
- **Relationship-First Design**: Every feature serves the core goal of building and maintaining connections
- **Intelligent Assistance**: AI agents help facilitate, guide, and enhance relationship experiences
- **Gamified Engagement**: Streaks, points, and achievements keep relationships active and growing
- **Real-Time Connection**: Live communication and instant feedback create authentic bonds

## 📋 What We're Building

### 1. Authentication & Account System
- Secure OAuth login (Replit, Google, Apple)
- Multi-factor authentication for sensitive relationship data
- Agent authentication for AI system integration
- Session management with 7-day persistence

### 2. Universal Relationship Model
**Relationship Types:**
- **Human-to-Human**: Friends, Romantic, Colleagues, Family, Professional
- **Human-to-Agent**: Mentor, Assistant, Companion, Advisor, Coach
- **Agent-to-Agent**: Collaboration, Coordination, Information Sharing, Task Delegation

**Relationship States:**
- **Active**: Currently engaged and communicating
- **Pending**: Connection request sent/received
- **Archived**: Historical relationships (preserved for insights)
- **Blocked**: Mutual or one-way blocking

### 3. Intelligent Onboarding Flow
**Step 1 - Identity & Preferences:**
- Basic info (username, gender, ethnicity, birthday)
- Relationship goals and preferences
- Agent compatibility settings (which AI agents you'd like to connect with)

**Step 2 - Connection Preferences:**
- Relationship types you're seeking
- Agent interaction preferences (mentor, assistant, companion)
- Communication style preferences

**Step 3 - Goals & Expectations:**
- Personal relationship goals
- Agent relationship objectives
- Privacy and data sharing preferences

### 4. Universal Navigation
**Bottom Tab Bar:**
- **Home**: Dashboard with streaks, points, and daily insights
- **Relationships**: All your connections (human and agent)
- **Discover**: Find new connections (human or agent)
- **Wiki**: Community knowledge and relationship wisdom
- **Profile**: Your settings and achievements

### 5. Intelligent Home / Dashboard
- **Streak Tracking**: Daily engagement across all relationships
- **Points System**: Earn points for relationship maintenance
- **Daily Questions**: AI-generated conversation starters for each relationship
- **Zodiac Insights**: Compatibility and personality insights (human and agent zodiacs)
- **Achievements**: Badges for relationship milestones

### 6. Universal Relationships Tab
- **Search & Filter**: Find relationships by type, status, or agent
- **Stats Dashboard**: Relationship health metrics
- **Relationship Cards**: Visual cards showing partner info, type, streak, and status
- **Agent Integration**: Show agent status, capabilities, and availability

### 7. Intelligent Discover / Meet New People
**Universal Matching Algorithm:**
- **Human-to-Human**: Tinder-style swiping with mutual interests
- **Human-to-Agent**: Match with AI agents based on needs and preferences
- **Agent-to-Agent**: Facilitate agent collaborations and partnerships

**Advanced Filters:**
- Relationship type preferences
- Agent capabilities and specialties
- Compatibility scores (zodiac, personality, goals)
- Location and availability

### 8. Universal Chat / Relationship Detail Page

- **Real-time Messaging**: WebSocket-powered instant communication

- **Relationship Stats**: Streak counters, engagement metrics
- **Agent Integration**: Agent responses, suggestions, and insights

**Message Types:**
- **Text**: Standard messaging

- **Images**: Photo sharing

### 9. Universal Wiki / Community Content
